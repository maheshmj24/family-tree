# Family Tree Application

## Quick Start (3 Steps)

1. **Extract** this folder to anywhere on your computer
2. **Double-click** on `Family-Tree.bat`
3. **Wait** for your browser to open with the application

That's it! Your family tree application is now running.

## What You Need

- Windows computer
- Google Chrome OR Microsoft Edge browser (most computers already have these)

## Troubleshooting

**If the app doesn't open:**
- Make sure you have Chrome or Edge installed
- Try right-clicking `Family-Tree-App.bat` and selecting "Run as administrator"

- Click "More info" â†’ "Run anyway" (this is normal for batch files)

## Using the Application

Once the app opens in your browser:

1. **Select a folder** for your family data when prompted
2. **Add people** using the "People" tab
3. **Add relationships** between family members
4. **Explore** your family tree in the "Network" and "Tree View" tabs

## Data Storage

Your family data is stored in the folder you select:
- `family-tree.json` - Your family information
- `photos/` folder - Family photos

## Need Help?

- Your data is saved automatically in the folder you choose
- You can backup your data by copying the folder
- The app works completely offline - no internet required
